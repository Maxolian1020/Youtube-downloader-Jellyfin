Playlists

Playlists live in the local SQLite DB. Pick one, then paste a single YouTube song link to add it directly to that playlist.

Import behavior: the selected playlist receives the song, the pasted video is saved as its download URL, metadata is looked up automatically, and YouTube thumbnails are not used as album art.
